The River Thames is without doubt one of the most famous rivers in Europe. It has been a source of life for centuries and its banks are now home to thriving towns and villages, rich countryside and historic landmarks. People have always been drawn to this river, either out of necessity, or purely to indulge and discover the delights it has to offer.

Located on a fabulous stretch of the river in South West London, Ditton Cruisers puts you at the helm of their well maintained day boats. Explore famous locations such as historic Hampton Court, the old market town of Kingston upon Thames, picturesque Hampton or Sunbury or simply find one of a number of idyllic spots to moor up and have a picnic…the choice is yours.

Relax and enjoy all the natural beauty that this river has to offer, picnic on its banks, moor up and visit any spot that takes your fancy or stop for a bite to eat at one of the many riverside pubs and restaurants. The choice is yours.

Where can
you stop?

Along the river there are various mooring points. Most of these are sign posted but bear in mind that some may make a charge.

What if it’s your first
time on a boat?

There is normally a lock keeper at every lock who will operate the lock for you. Just pull in slowly, place the mooring line around one of the bollards and hold the line securely until the gates open. You can then proceed out of the lock.
